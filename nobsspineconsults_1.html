Common questions

Before you reach out.

Is this emergency care?
No. This service is not designed for acute emergencies. If you are experiencing sudden loss of bowel or bladder control, rapidly progressing weakness, or severe trauma — go to an emergency room immediately. This service is for patients with chronic or ongoing spine conditions seeking clarity.
Is this a replacement for my local doctor?
No. This is an advisory second opinion. It complements your existing care — it does not replace your primary care physician or treating specialist. Think of it as a clear, unbiased read from a surgeon with no stake in what you decide next.
Do you prescribe medications?
No. This service does not include prescriptions, procedures, or ongoing treatment management. It is a consultation service focused exclusively on diagnosis review and treatment recommendations.
Can I pursue treatment anywhere?
Yes, absolutely. The recommendation you receive is yours to take anywhere. You are under no obligation to pursue treatment at miiSpine or any affiliated provider. The goal is to give you clarity — what you do with it is your choice.
